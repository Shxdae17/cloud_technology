Born December 8,1982, Onika Tanya Maraj-Petty,known professionally as Nicki Minaj, is a Trinidadian-born rapper, singer, and songwriter based in the United States. Often referred to as the "Queen of Rap", she is known for her musical versatility, animated flow in her rapping, alter egos, and influence in popular music. Growing up in Saint James district of Port of Spain, Trinidad and Tobago, Nicki grew up with an older brother named Jelani, a younger brother named Micaiah, and a younger sister named Ming. Minaj who grew up with her brother Jelani in Trini also grew up in a househould of 11 cousins. It wasnt until the age of five when Minaj, her mother, her father, and her brother moved to America on her mother green card and lived in their first house in South Jamaica Queens. Minaj later in life went on to audition for "LaGuardia High School of Music and Art and Performing Arts" which led her to become an actress in the off-broadway play "In Case you Forgot" after graduation. Struggling in her acting career, Nicki had to work at various jobs such as Red Lobster but was later fired for discourtesy to customers. She would later be fired from at least 15 other jobs for similar reasons. From 2004-2009, Minaj briefly signed with the group "Full Force", however she left Full force and started to upload her own music on her Myspace profile and sending several of her songs to people in the msuic industry. Originally adopting the stage name 'Nicki Miraj' Nicki later changed it to 'Nicki Minaj'. After releasing several mixtapes she was discovered by fellow rapper Lil Wayne and later signed ude his label 'Young Money Entertainment'. This later earned her a feature on the song "5 Star B****" with Gucci Mane and Trina. In 2010-2011, Nicki then her lead breakthrough with her debut album 'Pink Friday' which debuted number two and number one later on the Billboad 200 later that year. Nicki would then go on to relase several leading and hot sigles such as 'Super Bass' and 'Starships'. From there Nicki would go on to relase many more albums and is now about to release a 'Pink Friday 2' coming out November of 2023.
